How to use Vector Control Academy

1. Accounts

  • Use a valid email address and your Discord tag when registering.
  • Admins can promote users to mentors from the Admin dashboard.

2. Training flow

  1. Join the waiting list for Module 1.
  2. A mentor accepts you from the queue and conducts sessions.
  3. After the mentor marks a module as completed, you may queue for the next one.
  4. Failing Module 1 or 2 resets all progress – you must start again from Module 1.
  5. After all four modules are completed, you can request your Vector Control Academy rating.

3. Mentors

  • Mentors can pick trainees from queues and update module status (waiting / completed / failed).
  • Mentors and admins can create detailed session reports with subject, topic, comment and grade.
  • Mentors cannot remove other mentors from a trainee – only admins can kick from the queue.

4. Support & communication

  • Use the Support page to open a ticket and chat with staff.
  • Tickets can be closed by the creator, mentors or admins when the issue is resolved.

5. Disclaimer

This is a custom training and queue management tool created exclusively for Vector Control Academy. All terminology and examples are generic and for training purposes only.
